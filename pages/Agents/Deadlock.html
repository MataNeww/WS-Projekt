DEADLOCK

SENTINEL

Hailing from Morocco, Cypher is a vigilant sentinel who uses surveillance tools to gather intel on enemy movements, providing crucial information for his team.

ABILITIES

C – Trapwire
Equip and deploy a stealthed Trapwire, creating a tripwire between two walls that activates to reveal and restrain the first enemy who crosses it.
Q – Cyber Cage
Equip and toss out a remote-activated Cyber Cage device. Activate to create a zone that slows and impairs vision of players passing through it.
E – Spycam
Equip and deploy a Spycam, a remote camera that allows Cypher to view its feed or activate a dart to track enemies in its line of sight.
X – Neural Theft
Equip a Neural Theft Ultimate ability. Use on a dead enemy to reveal the location of all living enemies for a brief duration.
