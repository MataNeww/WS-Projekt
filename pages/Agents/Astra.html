ASTRA

CONTROLLER

Representing her home country of South Korea, Astra's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

ABILITIES

C – Nova Pulse
Unleash a star to detonate a Nova Pulse, blocking vision and concussing all players caught in the explosion.
Q – Gravity Well
Place Stars in Astral Form (X) to form a Gravity Well. Players in the area are pulled toward the center before it explodes, making all players still trapped inside fragile.
E – Nebula/Dissipate
Place Stars in Astral Form (X) to transform them into a Nebula (smokescreen) that can be reactivated later to Dissipate, or take a star back.
X – Cosmic Divide
Use (X) on a chosen location to call down a Cosmic Divide that spans the entire map and briefly winds its way through structures. Divide knocks away all Agents caught in the zone.
