SKYE

INITIATOR

Skye, the Australian initiator, commands a group of creatures to heal and support her team. She uses her menagerie to gain information and control the battlefield with a combination of healing and disruption.

ABILITIES

C – Trailblazer
EQUIP and send out a Tasmanian tiger trinket, which, when activated, increases in speed and explodes, concussing and damaging enemies in its radius.
Q – Regrowth
EQUIP and channel an AoE healing pool. The healing pool can be moved while channeling.
E – Seekers
EQUIP and send out three seekers to track down and nearsight the three nearest enemies.
X – Guiding Light
EQUIP and send out a hawk trinket that can be controlled in flight. When activated, the hawk explodes in a brilliant flash, blinding all players who see it.
