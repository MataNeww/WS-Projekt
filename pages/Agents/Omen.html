OMEN

CONTROLLER

Omen, the shadowy initiator, manipulates darkness to confuse and control his enemies. He's a master of teleportation and disruption.

ABILITIES

C – Shrouded Step
EQUIP a shadowy orb and see its range indicator. FIRE to send the orb to the marked location, creating a tether between the two points. RE-USE the ability while the tether is active to teleport to the marked location.
Q – Paranoia
EQUIP a blinding charge. FIRE to throw the charge to the marked location. Upon landing, the charge activates, blinding all looking at it. LEFT CLICK curves the charge left and RIGHT CLICK curves it right.
E – Dark Cover
EQUIP a smoke screen emitter. FIRE to throw the emitter to the marked location. RE-USE the ability while the emitter is active to curve it left or right.
X – From the Shadows
EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to begin teleporting to the selected location. While teleporting, Omen is phased and invulnerable. RIGHT CLICK to cancel the teleport.
