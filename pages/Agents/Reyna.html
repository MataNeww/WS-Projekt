REYNA

DUELIST

Reyna, the radiant duelist, uses her vampiric powers to become a relentless force on the battlefield. She thrives on eliminating enemies and absorbing their essence to enhance her abilities.

ABILITIES

C – Leer
EQUIP and cast a brief, forward-facing ethereal eye that, when active, Nearsights all looking at it. ACTIVATE the ability again to teleport the eye a short distance.
Q – Devour
Reyna consumes the life force of a killed enemy, healing for a duration. If the ability's healing exceeds her maximum health, she gains an overheal.
E – Dismiss
After a brief channel, Reyna enters a vulnerable state and then activates her Dismiss ability, becoming briefly invulnerable and invisible. If she gets a kill during this ability, the duration is refreshed.
X – Empress
Reyna consumes the life force of all enemies in her line of sight, becoming massively increased in speed, making her shots deadly, and gaining combat stim for the ability's duration.
