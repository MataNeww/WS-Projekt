BREACH

INITIATOR

Representing his home country of Sweden, Breach's powerful seismic abilities disrupt the battlefield, incapacitating enemies and opening up strategic opportunities for his team.

ABILITIES

C – Aftershock
Equip and fire a seismic charge into the ground. The blast does damage to players in the area and interrupts their actions.
Q – Flashpoint
Equip and throw a flash grenade. The grenade explodes after a delay or upon contact, blinding all looking at it.
E – Fault Line
Equip a seismic blast. Hold fire to increase the distance. Release to set off the quake, dazing all players in its zone.
X – Rolling Thunder
Equip a rolling thunder concussive charge. Fire to deploy the charge, which moves forward and dazes all players in its zone.
