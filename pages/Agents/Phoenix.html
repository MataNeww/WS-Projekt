PHOENIX

DUELIST

Phoenix, the fiery duelist, uses the power of fire to outmaneuver and outgun his opponents. He can heal himself and control the battlefield with flames.

ABILITIES

C – Blaze
EQUIP a flame wall. FIRE to create a line of fire that moves forward, creating a wall that blocks vision and damages anyone passing through. HOLD FIRE to curve the wall left and RIGHT CLICK to curve it right.
Q – Curveball
EQUIP a flash grenade. FIRE to throw the grenade. LEFT CLICK curves it left and RIGHT CLICK curves it right around corners.
E – Healing Flame
EQUIP a healing flame. FIRE to heal Phoenix. HOLD FIRE to heal over time.
X – Run It Back
EQUIP a fireball. FIRE to throw the fireball. HOLD FIRE to curve it left and RIGHT CLICK to curve it right. The fireball explodes upon impact, damaging and concussing all nearby enemies. If Phoenix is killed while his ultimate is active, or when it expires, he is reborn at the marked location with full health.
