RAZE

DUELIST

Raze, the explosive duelist, brings chaos and mayhem to the battlefield. She's armed with a collection of explosive devices that can disrupt enemies and clear tight spaces.

ABILITIES

C – Paint Shells
EQUIP a cluster grenade. FIRE to throw the grenade, which does damage and creates sub-munitions, each doing damage to anyone in their range.
Q – Blast Pack
EQUIP a satchel charge. FIRE to throw the charge on the ground. Upon activation, the satchel explodes, propelling anything nearby up into the air.
E – Boom Bot
EQUIP a Boom Bot. FIRE to deploy the bot, which travels in a straight line on the ground, bouncing off walls. The Boom Bot will lock on to any enemies in its frontal cone and chase them, exploding for damage if it reaches them.
X – Showstopper
EQUIP a rocket launcher. FIRE to launch a rocket in a straight line, dealing massive area-of-effect damage to enemies caught in the blast radius.
