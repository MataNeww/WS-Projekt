SOVA

INITIATOR

Sova, the initiator, uses recon and surveillance to gather information on enemy positions. He can reveal hidden enemies and disrupt their plans with a well-placed arsenal of abilities.

ABILITIES

C – Owl Drone
EQUIP and deploy an Owl Drone that can fly through the air, darting and tagging enemies in its view.
Q – Shock Bolt
EQUIP and fire a Shock Bolt that explodes upon impact, dealing damage to enemies caught in the blast radius.
E – Recon Bolt
EQUIP and deploy a Recon Bolt that can be controlled in flight. After deploying, activate the ability again to transform the bolt into a sonar emitter, revealing nearby enemies.
X – Hunter's Fury
EQUIP a bow and fire up to three deadly energy blasts. Each blast can travel through walls and damages any players caught in its path. The ability can be curbed mid-fire.
