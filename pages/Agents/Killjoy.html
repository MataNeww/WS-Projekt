KILLJOY

SENTINEL

Killjoy, the German sentinel, is a genius inventor who uses her deployable gadgets to secure areas and disrupt enemy plans. She can control the battlefield with her array of inventions.

ABILITIES

C – Nanoswarm
EQUIP and throw a grenade that, upon activation, deploys a swarm of nanobots dealing damage to players caught in the area.
Q – Alarmbot
EQUIP and deploy a bot that hunts down enemies in a straight line. Upon reaching an enemy, the bot explodes, applying a Vulnerable debuff to enemies in the blast radius.
E – Turret
EQUIP and deploy a turret that fires at enemies in a 180-degree cone. The turret can be manually activated to fire a rapid burst of bullets.
X – Lockdown
EQUIP a device that detains all enemies in a large radius after a long wind-up. The detained enemies are unable to move or use abilities during the detention.
