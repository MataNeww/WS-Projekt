SAGE

SENTINEL

Sage, the healing sentinel, supports her team with powerful healing abilities and crowd control. She can reshape the battlefield with her control over the flow of combat.

ABILITIES

C – Barrier Orb
EQUIP a Barrier Orb. FIRE places a solid wall. ALT FIRE rotates the targeter.
Q – Healing Orb
EQUIP a Healing Orb. FIRE with the target to activate its healing properties. ALT FIRE while Sage is damaged to activate a self-heal.
E – Slow Orb
EQUIP a Slow Orb. FIRE to throw the orb, creating a field that slows players who walk on it.
X – Resurrection
EQUIP a Resurrection ability. FIRE with the target corpse to begin resurrecting them. After a brief channel, the target is brought back to life with full health.
