VIPER

CONTROLLER

Viper, the toxic controller, dominates the battlefield with her deadly chemical arsenal. She controls the flow of the game, creating toxic environments that give her team an edge.

ABILITIES

C – Snake Bite
EQUIP a chemical thrower. FIRE to lob a canister that erupts into a damaging pool of acid on impact with the ground.
Q – Poison Cloud
EQUIP a gas emitter. FIRE to deploy a poison cloud that obscures vision on impact with the floor. HOLD FIRE to disperse the cloud, allowing Viper to redeploy it elsewhere.
E – Toxic Screen
EQUIP a gas emitter. FIRE to deploy a long line of gas emitters. RE-USE the ability to create a tall wall of toxic gas at the cost of fuel. This wall can be rotated before placement.
X – Viper's Pit
EQUIP a chemical sprayer. FIRE to unleash a decay-inducing mist. While in the mist, Viper gains heightened senses, allowing her to see and target enemies through walls. Viper's Pit lasts as long as her fuel allows.
