BRIMSTONE

CONTROLLER

Hailing from the United States, Brimstone is a powerful controller who uses incendiary devices to control the battlefield, providing strategic advantages for his team.

ABILITIES

C – Stim Beacon
Equip and deploy a Stim Beacon. All players in the area benefit from increased fire rate.
Q – Incendiary
Equip and launch an incendiary grenade that explodes into a brief fire on impact with the floor, dealing damage over time to players caught in the area.
E – Sky Smoke
Equip and throw a Sky Smoke Grenade, which expands into a brief vision-blocking sphere upon impact with the floor.
X – Orbital Strike
Equip a tactical map. Fire to launch a lingering orbital strike laser at the selected location, dealing damage over time to players caught in the area.
